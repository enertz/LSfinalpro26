IIT Bombay Grading Policy

Letter Grades and Grade Points:
AA - 10 points (Outstanding)
AB - 9 points (Excellent)
BB - 8 points (Very Good)
BC - 7 points (Good)
CC - 6 points (Average)
CD - 5 points (Below Average)
DD - 4 points (Marginal Pass)
FF - 0 points (Fail)

CPI (Cumulative Performance Index) is the weighted average of grade points across all courses.
SPI (Semester Performance Index) is the weighted average of grade points in a single semester.
A student must maintain a minimum CPI of 5.0 to avoid academic probation.
A student with CPI below 4.0 after two semesters may be asked to leave the institute.
Courses with FF grade must be repeated. The new grade replaces FF in CPI calculation.
A student can repeat a course to improve grade only if they scored DD or below.
