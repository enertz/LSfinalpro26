IIT Bombay Academic Support and Student Services

Faculty Advisor System:
- Every student is assigned a Faculty Advisor (FA)
- FA must be consulted for course registration issues
- FA approval required for credit overload requests
- Students must meet FA at least once per semester

Academic Support:
- Institute provides tutoring support through Teaching Assistants (TAs)
- TAs hold office hours for doubt clearing
- Academic Resource Centre provides study materials
- Library is open 24x7 during exam periods

Attendance Policy:
- Minimum 75% attendance required in each course
- Students below 75% attendance are deregistered (DX grade)
- DX grade is equivalent to FR for CPI calculation
- Medical leave is counted separately with documentation

Leave Policy:
- Students must inform their Faculty Advisor before taking leave
- Leave form must be submitted to the academic office
- Internship leave during semester requires Dean Academic approval
- Maximum 10 days leave allowed during semester without penalty

Scholarship and Financial Aid:
- Merit scholarship for students with CPI above 8.0
- Need-based scholarship available through Student Welfare office
- Institute assistantship available for PG students
- Emergency financial assistance through Dean Student Affairs office
