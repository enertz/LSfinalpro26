IIT Bombay Course Registration Rules

Registration Process:
Students register for courses through the Academic Portal (ASC) at the start of each semester.
Course registration opens one week before semester start.
Students can add courses in the first two weeks of the semester.
Students can drop courses without grade penalty in the first two weeks.
After the add/drop deadline, withdrawal is allowed until mid-October (Sem 1) or mid-March (Sem 2).
A withdrawn course appears as W on the transcript and does not affect CPI.

Credit Limits:
Minimum credits per semester: 12
Maximum credits per semester: 60 (with faculty advisor approval)
Normal load: 36-48 credits per semester

Prerequisites:
Students must have completed prerequisites before registering for a course.
The system automatically checks prerequisites during registration.
Prerequisite overrides require written approval from the course instructor.

Backlog Courses:
Students with FF grade must re-register for the course in a subsequent semester.
Backlog courses count toward the semester credit limit.
