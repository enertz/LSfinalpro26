IIT Bombay Examination Rules

General Rules:
- Students must carry their identity card to all examinations
- Entry is not allowed 30 minutes after the exam begins
- Students must be seated 10 minutes before the exam starts
- Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited

Mid-Semester Examination:
- Duration: 1.5 hours typically
- Covers syllabus up to the date of exam
- Weightage: typically 20-30% of total grade

End-Semester Examination:
- Duration: 3 hours
- Covers entire syllabus
- Weightage: typically 40-50% of total grade

Malpractice:
- Any form of cheating results in FR grade in the course
- Repeat offence can lead to rustication
- Use of unfair means is reported to the Academic Disciplinary Committee

Medical Exemption:
- Students who miss exams due to medical reasons must submit medical certificate within 3 days
- Make-up exam is granted at the discretion of the instructor
- Medical certificate must be from institute hospital or approved hospital

Grade Moderation:
- Instructors may moderate grades after end-semester exams
- Moderation is done to ensure fair distribution
- Students can apply for re-evaluation within 1 week of grade declaration

Medical Exemption for Missed Exams:
Students who miss an exam due to medical reasons must submit a medical certificate within 3 days to the academic office. A make-up exam may be granted at the instructor's discretion. Without valid documentation, a missed exam results in zero marks for that component.
